About Us

“Student Society”
ACES is a Community of Computer Engineering Students established in 2070 B.S with an aim of all round development of students and build a better foundation for their carrier.

Training

We provide training to students from 1st to 4th year of Bachelors in Computer Engineering.

Competition

We organize various competitions for students to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Podcast

We organize podcast sessions with experts in technical field for students .

Techfest

We organize Techfest every year for students to showcase their skills.

Our Aim

Skill Development

We conduct training and workshops on various technologies and skills. We also organize competition among students to enhance their skills. We organize podcast event to help students to learn from industry experts.

Support

We provide support to students in their projects and also help them to set their skills. We also provide support to students in their career and help them to choose the right path.

Competition

We organize various competitions among students to enhance their skills and to provide them a platform to showcase their talent. We also organize hackathons and different technical events to help students to learn and to enhance their skills.

Our Upcoming Events

Hackathon

Each team consists of 3-4 members. A theme will be chosen by the organizer at the start. There will be an hour of discussion and preparation where teams will have to decide on the type of product they're going to build. It will be followed by 36 hours of continuous coding with 4 food breaks in between.



Treasure Hunt

Hints will be hidden inside the college premises. Team of participants search for the hint and crack the code. The team that submits the token fastest will win.



Capture The Flag

Flags/Vulnerabilities are hidden throughout various websites. Participants will have to discover as many flags in the given time. The winner shall be the player that discovers the most flags in the shortest amount of time.
